Project Summary

This proposal is for a targeted patch repair and temporary stabilization of the asphalt shingle roof. The current roof system is nearing the end of its functional life; this work is strictly intended as a temporary measure to address localized buckling and critical transition gaps.

Investment Detail

Shingle Stabilization & Transition Seal
  • Safety & Setup: Ground-anchored fall protection for steep-slope access.
  • Detailed Shingle Remediation:
    • Careful release of existing shingle sealant bonds to prevent asphalt tearing.
    • Identification and removal of "proud" nails causing surface buckling.
    • Mechanical re-fastening of affected courses in the proper strike zone.
  • Adhesive Stabilization: High-viscosity roofing cement application to bond tabs flush.
  • Perimeter Sealing: Elastomeric sealant at roof-to-siding transition to prevent wicking.
