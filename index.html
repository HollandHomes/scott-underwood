Project Summary

This proposal is for a targeted patch repair and temporary stabilization of the asphalt shingle roof. The current roof system is nearing the end of its functional life; this work is strictly intended as a temporary measure to address localized buckling and critical transition gaps.

Investment Detail

Shingle Stabilization & Transition Seal
  • Safety Setup: Implementation of a ground-anchored fall protection system for secure access to the steep-slope pitch.
  • Shingle Remediation:
    • Careful release of existing shingle sealant bonds to prevent asphalt tearing.
    • Identification and removal of "proud" or incorrectly driven nails causing surface buckling.
    • Mechanical re-fastening of affected courses in the proper manufacturer strike zone.
  • Adhesive Stabilization: Application of high-viscosity roofing cement to the underside of all buckled shingle tabs to manually bond them flush against the steep pitch.
  • Perimeter Sealing: Application of professional-grade elastomeric sealant to the roof-to-siding transition to prevent water wicking behind wall boards.
